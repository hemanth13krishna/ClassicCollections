What We Do

Full-Spectrum
Garment Solutions

Every stage of label manufacturing handled in-house at our New Delhi facility. Faster turnaround, tighter quality control.

01
Garment Label Design & Production
Custom woven, printed, and embroidered labels to exact brand specs. Damask, satin, and taffeta weaves with 30+ thread colour options.
02
Premium Leather Patches
Full-grain and top-grain genuine leather patches with debossing, embossing, laser engraving, and foil stamping. Standard and custom sizing.
03
Quality Testing & Compliance
Full in-house testing: colorfastness, tensile strength, adhesion, and regulatory compliance. Every international brand standard met before shipping.
04
Garment Printing
Direct-to-garment, screen printing, and heat transfer services. Plastisol and water-based inks, up to 12 spot colours with specialty finishes.
05
Hang Tags & Packaging
Premium hang tags and retail packaging on 400gsm+ board. Foil stamping, spot UV, custom die-cut shapes, and full barcode integration.
06
Brand Partnership Programs
Dedicated account management for approved brand partners. Streamlined ordering, brand asset management, and priority production scheduling.
How It Works

From Brief to
Delivery

Our proven five-stage process eliminates errors and ensures every order arrives on time, on spec, and to brand standard — every time.

01
Brief & Spec
Submit requirements, artwork, and brand guidelines directly with your account manager.
02
Design & Proof
Our design team creates digital and physical proofs for brand review before production.
03
Material Testing
All substrates tested against brand and regulatory specifications in our in-house QC lab.
04
Production
Manufacturing begins on approval with live status updates throughout the process.
05
QC & Delivery
Final inspection, packing to brand spec, and tracked delivery with full documentation.
